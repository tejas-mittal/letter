My Dearest Aakansha

Since that moment, my days have felt brighter, lighter, and filled with a joy I never knew I needed. Your laughter—so pure and carefree, like a child’s—wraps around my heart and makes everything feel right. You have this way of making life feel simpler, calmer, like a deep breath in the middle of a storm.

I think you are close to understanding the true meaning of life—letting others talk without bothering yourself, enjoying every second of it without dwelling too much on the past or worrying about the future, staying true to your heart, and never hurting others in any way. Most importantly, you value making real heart-to-heart connections in this fake, social media-dominated world, where such qualities are rapidly disappearing. You have an exceptional talent for creating mandala art that I absolutely adore—it effortlessly takes my heart away. In many ways, I see you as a reflection of these values, resonating with the same essence as myself.

At the end of the day, you will always be my girl and I am in love with you So, here I am, asking you from the deepest corner of my heart: Will you let me spend the days ahead loving you making them most?

Forever yours,
